The Rock

The Rock, near University Hall, is one of the best-known landmarks on the Evanston campus.

Students began painting the Rock in the 1950s, first as a prank, then as an accepted avenue of expression. Now, almost every night, a different group paints a message or symbols promoting a particular cause or event.

Quick Facts:

  • Date: 1902
